Material Picker

How to use the material picker tool in nanos world Forge

The Material Picker tool lets you quickly identify materials by clicking on them directly in the viewport. It's especially useful when working with meshes that use multiple materials and you need to find a specific one.

How to Use​

To use it, simply click on the picker icon in the interface, then click on any surface in your level that has a material applied. The tool will automatically detect the selected material.

Once a material is picked, you can either browse to it in the content browser or edit it directly in the material editor.
